Get Started

Smart Task Management That Helps You Get Things Done

Done List isn't just a to-do app—it's your intelligent productivity partner that automatically suggests how to accomplish your tasks.

AI Auto-Suggestions

When you add a task, our AI automatically analyzes it and suggests step-by-step instructions on how to complete it. No more wondering where to start—get actionable breakdowns instantly.

Smart Subtasks

Break down complex tasks into manageable steps. AI suggestions automatically create subtasks that you can check off as you progress. When all subtasks are done, the main task completes automatically.

Calendar Integration

Assign tasks to specific dates and view them in a beautiful calendar view. Plan your week, track deadlines, and never miss an important task.

Everyday Tasks

Mark tasks as "everyday" to create recurring routines. Perfect for daily habits, weekly reviews, or any task that needs regular attention.

Regenerate Suggestions

Not happy with the AI suggestions? Click "Generate Better Version" to get alternative step-by-step approaches for completing your task.

Clean & Calm UI

A beautifully designed interface with a soothing color palette that helps you focus. Filter tasks, track progress, and stay organized without the overwhelm.
